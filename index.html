Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Money Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Financial Data

We collect the following financial information that you voluntarily provide:

• Receipt images and scanned data (merchant name, date, total amount, items purchased)

• Income records (amount, date, category, description)

• Expense categories and budgets

• Currency preferences and exchange rate data

2.2 Image Data

When you take photos of receipts or select images from your gallery, we process these images using our OCR (Optical Character Recognition) service to extract text and financial information. Images are processed by OpenAI Vision API to recognize and extract data from receipts.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect limited device information necessary for app functionality:

• Device type and operating system version

• App version and usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

• To provide receipt scanning and expense tracking functionality

• To generate financial analytics, reports, and forecasts

• To process images through OCR services to extract receipt data

• To improve app performance and user experience

• To send you notifications and reminders (with your consent)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your financial data, including receipts, incomes, budgets, and analytics, is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not maintain a central database of your information. Your data never leaves your device except when:

• You export data (CSV, JSON, PDF) and share it yourself

• You use the OCR feature, which requires sending images to OpenAI Vision API for text recognition

4.2 OCR Processing

When you scan a receipt, the image is temporarily sent to OpenAI Vision API for processing. OpenAI's Privacy Policy applies to this data transmission. We do not store images on external servers, and images are not retained by our service after processing.

4.3 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

• Local encryption of sensitive data

• Secure API connections (HTTPS) for OCR processing

• No cloud storage or external databases

• Regular security updates and patches

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 OpenAI Vision API

We use OpenAI Vision API for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract text and data from receipt images. When you scan a receipt, the image is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data:

https://openai.com/privacy/

5.2 Exchange Rate Services

We may use third-party exchange rate APIs to provide currency conversion functionality. These services do not receive any personal or financial data from your device.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal or financial information with third parties, except:

• OCR Processing: Receipt images are sent to OpenAI Vision API solely for text extraction

• Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

• User Export: You can export your data at any time using the export feature

7. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

• Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app

• Export: You can export all your data in CSV, JSON, or PDF formats at any time

• Deletion: You can delete individual receipts, incomes, or budgets at any time

• App Deletion: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

• Notifications: You can control notification settings in the app settings

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

• Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app

• Updating the "Last updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted.
